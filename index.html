Signature Dish

Golden Chicken Manchurian

A bold fusion of Chinese technique and Indian spice, our Golden Chicken Manchurian is crispy, juicy, and deeply flavorful. Marinated chicken bites are wok-tossed in a rich soy-garlic sauce, spiked with green chilies and bell peppers for that signature sizzle and zing. Finished with a hint of honey and toasted sesame, it's served with fragrant fried rice or hakka noodles. A modern classic that bridges tradition and indulgence — only at Belora.Golden Chicken Manchurian is a majestic fusion of Indo-Chinese heritage, crafted for those who appreciate bold flavors with a refined presentation.
